PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy and data handling

SERA is designed with privacy and responsible data handling in mind. Guest and customer information should be used only to provide the agreed service, support approved escalations and meet legitimate operational or legal requirements.

Information that may be processed

  • Customer and property contact information
  • Approved property instructions and escalation rules
  • Guest messages required to provide support
  • Service, onboarding and support records

Final policy must define GDPR rights, controller and processor roles, lawful bases, international transfers, security measures, data-retention periods, deletion procedures, complaint routes and contact details.
