FAQ

Questions, answered clearly.

What is SERA?

An AI-powered WhatsApp guest concierge for short-term rental hosts and property managers.

Does the guest need an app?

No. Guests communicate through WhatsApp.

Basic or Professional?

Basic uses structured flows and approved information. Professional adds a Claude-powered conversational AI guest agent.

Does SERA replace the host?

No. It handles repetitive questions and escalates matters needing human action.

What happens in emergencies?

SERA follows approved procedures and directs guests to emergency services where immediate danger exists.

Can SERA manage multiple properties?

Yes, with separate approved information for each property.

How long does setup take?

Internal configuration normally takes up to two business days after all information is received. WhatsApp activation targets 3–10 business days, depending on Meta approval.

When does the trial begin?

When the agreed pilot is operational. No monthly subscription is charged during the trial.

Are prices inclusive of VAT?

No. Prices exclude VAT.

Can I cancel?

Cancellation is governed by the final approved terms. Review them before subscribing.
